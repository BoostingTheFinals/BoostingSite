Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Is your boosting service safe?

Yes. Manual play only. Zero bans. 60+ orders completed safely.

Do you boost console accounts (PlayStation, Xbox)?

Account Driver: Steam-linked only. Selfplay: All platforms.

Can I play on my account during the boost (Account Driver)?

We strongly recommend against playing during active boosting for security and efficiency reasons.

What is your refund policy?

Before start: Full refund. During: Partial. Not completed: Full refund.

Can I chat with my booster during the process?

Absolutely! Each order comes with a private DM so you can get instant updates, match screenshots, and talk directly with your booster anytime.

Do you offer coaching or just boosting?

We focus on boosting services. However, with Selfplay you can learn strategies and improve while playing alongside our pros.

How does the payment process work?

Contact us via email or Discord. Payment is handled outside the website via person-to-person transfer, and once it's confirmed we begin the boost.

What if I lose games after the boost?

We deliver you to your target rank safely. What happens after is up to you, but we recommend playing carefully if you want to maintain your new rank.

How will the live stream work during the boost?

We stream the boost through a fully anonymous Twitch account. Our booster also uses anonymous in-game settings, including the option that displays their in-game name as “Me.” Additionally, the screen is hidden after every match so the lobby, player names, and teammates are not visible.
