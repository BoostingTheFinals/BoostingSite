The Finals Boosting FAQ

Is boosting safe?

Yes, we use no cheats, no software, and no scripts. Everything is played manually by professional boosters.

How long does boosting take?

Most boosts are completed within a few hours up to one day depending on your target rank.

Do you offer DuoQ/Selfplay?

Yes. You can play alongside a booster instead of giving account access.

How do I track progress?

We update you on Discord or email after major milestones.
