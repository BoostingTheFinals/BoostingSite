Is your boosting service safe?

Yes. Manual play only. Zero bans. 30+ orders completed safely. All boosting is done by professional players with no cheats or scripts.

How long does boosting take?

Most boosts are completed within 24-48 hours depending on your current rank and target. We provide time estimates during the order process.

Do you boost console accounts (PlayStation, Xbox)?

Account Driver: Steam-linked accounts only. Selfplay: All platforms supported.

Can I play on my account during the boost?

We strongly recommend against playing during active boosting for security and efficiency reasons. With Selfplay, you play together with our team.

What is your refund policy?

Before boost starts: Full refund. During boost: Partial refund based on progress. If we don't complete as promised: Full refund guaranteed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Wise, Binance (crypto), Revolut, and bank transfers. Payment is required before starting the boost.

Do you offer coaching or just boosting?

We focus on boosting services. However, with Selfplay you can learn strategies and improve while playing alongside our pros.

Can I chat with my booster during the process?

Absolutely! Each order comes with a private DM so you can get instant updates, match screenshots, and talk directly with your booster anytime.

What if I lose games after the boost?

We deliver you to your target rank safely. What happens after is up to you, but we recommend playing carefully if you want to maintain your new rank.
