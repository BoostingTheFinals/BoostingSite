Why players get stuck in Gold

Gold is the most common rank where players stall out. The reasons are almost always the same:

  • Solo queue teammates who chase kills and ignore the cashout
  • Inconsistent win rates due to matchmaking variance, not skill
  • Getting matched against premade stacks as a solo player
